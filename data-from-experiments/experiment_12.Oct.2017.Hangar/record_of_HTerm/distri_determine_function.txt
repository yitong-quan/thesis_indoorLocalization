matlab function:

lillietest
	vector x comes from a distribution in the normal family

jbtest
	x comes from a normal distribution with an unknown mean and variance

kstest
	x comes from a standard normal distribution

vartest(x,v)
	x comes from a normal distribution with variance v

ttest
	x comes from a normal distribution with mean equal to zero and unknown variance

ztest(x,m,sigma)
	comes from a normal distribution with mean m and a standard deviation sigma
